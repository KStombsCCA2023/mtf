If you start crocheting according to Bella's video, you may notice she is from the UK, and the hook sizes and stich terms are different in the UK then they are in the USA. To help clarify the difference between the basic terms she uses and the terms you'll find in the Hobby Lobby and other crafting stores, I have made a table for you. You're welcome.
